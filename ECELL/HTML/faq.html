FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is ECELL DCRUSTM ?

E-Cell DCRUSTM is the entrepreneurial community of DCRUST providing budding entrepreneurs and enthusiasts a nurturing ground to ideate innovate and incubate their startups and bring out the Steve Jobs inside them!

How Can I Join ?

Get in touch with any of the coordinators in the Team page of E-Cell Website and we will get you added to our community. Complete your assigned tasks diligently and voila! you’re now a member of this growing community!
