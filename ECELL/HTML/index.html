Who We Are ?

E-Cell DCRUST Murthal is an institute body run by the students of Deenbandhu Chhotu Ram University of science and technology (DCRUST) Murthal devoted to acting as a symbiotic link between the entrepreneurs of E-Cell and the existing startup ecosystem as well as acting as a hub where all the startups can meet ,collaborate and elevate!
